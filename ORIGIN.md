# Diagram Sources

This document records the source of the repository diagrams and numerical arrangements. Rotation plots, spectra, and analysis figures are derived outputs, not separate historical diagrams.

The exact diagrams in this repository were actually transcribed from *Gusuryak* (九數略). Damaged or uncertain readings were restored by comparing the source layout, sequences, and sum constraints. A failed web search is not evidence that a diagram was invented here.

## Short note

Chinese provenance is stated only for the Hetu/wuxing tradition, the 3x3 Luoshu record, and Yang Hui's related higher-order examples, where the evidence is explicit. Shared Chinese characters, names, or terminology alone are not used to infer a transmission route. The exact diagrams listed here are Joseon *Gusuryak* source material.
