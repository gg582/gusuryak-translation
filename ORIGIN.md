# Diagram Sources

This document records the source attribution used by this repository for the
diagrams and numerical arrangements. It is not a complete history of origins or
transmission. Rotation plots, spectra, and analysis figures are derived outputs,
not separate historical diagrams.

The project attributes the exact diagrams in this repository to its *Gusuryak*
(九數略) source material. Damaged or uncertain readings were restored by
comparing the source layout, sequences, and sum constraints. This is a project
source attribution, not a claim that the surviving printed witnesses establish a
worldwide first, a direct transmission route, or conceptual priority.

## Short note

Chinese textual background is mentioned only for the Hetu/wuxing tradition, the
3x3 Luoshu record, and Yang Hui's related higher-order examples. These are
contextual precedents, not proven transmission chains for the exact repository
layouts. Shared Chinese characters, names, or terminology alone are not used to
infer a route. The exact diagrams listed here follow the project's attribution
to Joseon *Gusuryak* source material; broader comparison with other surviving
classical works remains outside this inventory.
