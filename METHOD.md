## Transcription Methodology for Gusu-ryak

1. First, every diagram is transcribed by hand. Handwritten notes are then fed into an OCR program, and any misrecognized numbers are corrected manually.

2. Second, when the numbers in the source text clearly produce an error after following the commentary's computational procedure, the numbers are corrected and the scope of the correction is recorded.

3. Third, the transcribed materials are extended from the original problem to extract basic properties and shared characteristics.

4. Fourth, additional exploration is carried out with Python 3-based solvers.
