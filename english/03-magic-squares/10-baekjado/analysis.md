# Baekjado (Hundred Sons Diagram / 百子圖)

Order: 10×10

[Based on Reconstructed Version] A reconstructed 10x10 number arrangement (semi-magic square). This corrects the errors in the corners (8 cells at both ends of rows 5 to 8) caused by transcription mistakes in the original Baekjado by the Song Dynasty mathematician Yang Hui. This version restores the original consistent semi-magic square structure and symmetric patterns of the tens place, where all horizontal row sums and vertical column sums accurately satisfy the magic constant of 505.
