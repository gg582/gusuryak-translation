## Jungsang-yong-gudo (象上用九圖) — Source Text Decoding

### 1. Diagram Title and Basic Composition
> **象上用九圖 三十三子作 七十二子用**
* **Reading:** Jungsang-yong-gudo, samship-sam-ja-jak, chil-sip-i-ja-yong
* **Interpretation:** Jungsang-yong-gudo is constructed from 33 numbers and uses a total of 72 positions (computational usages), counting overlaps.

---

### 2. Mathematical Rules and Sums
> **斜直四行 周圍四重 各得一百四十七數**
* **Reading:** Sa-jik-sa-haeng, ju-wi-sa-jung, gak-deuk-il-baek-sa-sip-chil-su
* **Interpretation:** The four slanted/straight rows and the four surrounding rings each obtain the sum **147**.

> **本積五百六十一**
* **Reading:** Bon-jeok-o-baek-yuk-sip-il
* **Interpretation:** The original accumulated sum of the placed numbers 1 through 33, without duplication, is **561**.

> **演積一千一百四十八**
* **Reading:** Yeon-jeok-il-cheon-il-baek-sa-sip-pal
* **Interpretation:** The total sum of all lines and rings, counting duplicated positions according to the computation, is **1148**.

---

### 3. Background Principle
> **右五圖 天地成數 變化之數**
* **Reading:** U-o-do, cheon-ji-seong-su, byeon-hwa-ji-su
* **Interpretation:** The five diagrams on the right are numbers that embody the *completion numbers* of Heaven and Earth (成數) and the principle of cosmic change.

> **用五用六用七 係新定**
* **Reading:** Yong-o, yong-ryuk, yong-chil, gye-sin-jeong
* **Interpretation:** The computational rules that use 5, use 6, and use 7 belong to a newly established system.
